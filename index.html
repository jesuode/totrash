About

I'm Joel Ehigiator, a Computer Science graduate from the University of Benin with a strong passion for web technologies and digital product design. My journey began in 2021 with front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), and I quickly expanded into back-end development to become a well-rounded full-stack developer.

Alongside development, I cultivated a deep interest in UI/UX and product design, allowing me to bridge creativity with functionality. I specialize in crafting intuitive user experiences and scalable web solutions, blending code and design to deliver complete digital products.

I've built and refined multiple personal and client projects, including portfolio websites, business sites, and custom web applications, many of which are showcased on GitHub and Behance. These experiences have strengthened my ability to take a project from initial concept to final deployment, while maintaining high standards of quality and usability.
